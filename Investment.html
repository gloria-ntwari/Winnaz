CORPORATE VIDEO

Who are we

We want to have a timeline here. Winnaz will be 10 years this year and we want to add a timeline design.

You can work with the following milestones as a start:

  • April 2013: Thijs Boer visits Rwanda for MSc research in Development Economics, focusing on entrepreneurship among potato farmers. Sparks curiosity about private sector development and challenges in processing and value addition.
  • January 2014:Post-research, Thijs begins sourcing refurbished machinery from the Netherlands and raises €100,000 for investment. Receives guidance from Hoeksche Chips on machinery selection and crisp production.
  • Early 2015: Business officially starts. Winnaz begins operations with a commitment to quality and around-the-clock work to establish a strong brand presence in Eastern Africa and the Great Lake region.

Ambition & growth

We are on track to become the market leader in the Netherlands with our innovative soft drinks by 2028. Our ambition? A 5% market share in the premium soft drink market. With your help, we can double our production capacity and introduce a wide range of new flavours. Our turnover target for 2028 is €10 million, with a strong focus on sustainability and healthy alternatives.

Fair and Sustainable

At De Amsterdamse Frisdrank Fabriek, sustainability is key. We use natural ingredients, with minimal impact on the environment. Our packaging is 100% recyclable and we continue to innovate to reduce our ecological footprint. All this without compromising on taste or quality.

Why invest?

Your investment will help us open new production lines, increase our impact, and introduce fair soft drinks to a wider audience. Together with you, we can take De Amsterdamse Frisdrank Fabriek to the next level and shape a sustainable future for the soft drink industry.

MISSION & VISION

Mission

At The Amsterdam Soft Drink Factory, our mission is to bring soft drinks back to their core pure, honest, and without artificial additives. We want to make a positive impact by combining sustainable choices with authentic flavors, straight from the heart of Amsterdam.

Vision

We aim to be a factory in which every consumer has access to the soft drinks, produced with respect for people and the environment. By continuing to innovate and grow, we want to become the market leader in sustainable soft drinks, both nationally and internationally.

AMSTERDAM SOFT DRINK FACTORY

Who are we?

At the Amsterdamse Frisdrank Fabriek we specialize in creating unique, healthy craft sodas. With years of expertise in natural ingredients and flavor development, we work on soft drinks that are not only delicious, but also pure and honest. In our laboratory we develop innovative flavors and refine existing recipes to make them even better.

Team of Experts

Our team consists of passionate professionals who have mastered the art of making soft drinks to perfection. Led by founder Tim de Hoon, an expert in the food industry, and a dedicated team of professionals, we have built a strong foundation for sustainable growth. Every day, our production team strives for the highest quality.

What do we do?

Our mission is to capture the purity of life in every bottle. Every drink we make is an experience of pure and honest taste.
